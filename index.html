How I Think as a Product Analyst

01

Start from User Problem Definition

Every initiative begins with a clearly articulated user pain point ‚Äî not an assumption or a feature request.

02

Quantify Impact Before Solution

Size the opportunity with data. Prioritize where evidence shows the highest leverage, not where opinions are loudest.

03

Validate with Data

Hypotheses are cheap. I test assumptions through analytics, user research, and instrumentation before committing resources.

04

Iterate with Experiments

Ship small, measure fast, learn continuously. Incremental improvement compounds into transformational outcomes.

05

Balance Feasibility, Viability & Desirability

Great products live at the intersection of what users want, what the business needs, and what engineering can deliver.
